Threats to Prosecute Trump After His Term are Dangerous

In the wake of Special Counsel Mueller’s much-anticipated report on Russian interference into the 2016 election, some Democrats (and lone Republican Rep. Justin Amash (R-MI)) have called for formal impeachment proceedings against President Trump, despite Mueller’s surprisingly inconclusive findings. Speaker Nancy Pelosi, wary of the political downside presented by impeaching Trump without ammo from Mueller, not to mention broad bipartisan support, has so far bucked her fellow caucus members.
